ABOUT

Sumukh is a software developer, specializing in Android. He currently works at Meta in New York City.

He most recently worked at Genius, developing their Android app. Prior to that, he worked on developing new products and experiments in a startup-like environment, performing a hybrid product management and software development role at Yahoo. At Yahoo, he also contributed to solving emerging technical problems in search, artificial intelligence, and messaging.

A graduate of the Viterbi School of Engineering at the University of Southern California, Sumukh has a bachelor's degree in Computer Science with an emphasis in video game development. He has been working at various tech companies in the Silicon Valley since 2009.

Sumukh looks forward to learning new languages, environments, and tools, and growing his skill set. He is interested in opportunities that allow him to push his limits both technically and creatively.

When he's not working on tech projects, Sumukh spends his time in his personal music studio composing, recording, and producing music under various pseudonyms in various styles.
